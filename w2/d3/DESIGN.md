# W2-D3 Design

Endpoint `POST /incident` cua em phuc vu toan bo pipeline AIOps thay vi chi serve rieng mot model. Request vao la batch alert, duoc Pydantic validate truoc khi vao logic chinh. Sau do service chay correlation theo huong cua W2-D1: session window `gap_sec=120` va topology grouping `max_hop=2` tren service graph. Tu danh sach cluster tao ra, service chon cluster lon nhat lam primary incident. Cluster nay duoc dua qua layer RCA cua W2-D2, ket hop `PageRank` tren subgraph voi `timestamp_score` theo ti le `0.6 / 0.4`, roi retrieval top-3 incident lich su de gan `class`, `recommended_actions`, va `similar_incidents`.

Ve latency budget, em ky vong phan ton thoi gian nhat la RCA retrieval va serialize response, con validation va correlation tren dataset 20 alert la nhe. Em da them middleware de gan header `X-Response-Time-Ms` cho moi request, nham do duoc p50/p99 khi benchmark. Khi input tang 10x, phan correlation va RCA graph se scale gan nhu linear theo so alert va so service trong cluster, trong khi load graph/history la fixed cost vi duoc cache o module-level tu luc service khoi dong.

Production concern ma em uu tien la fault tolerance va readiness. Em tach `/healthz` va `/readyz`: `healthz` chi tra loi process con song, con `readyz` check graph, incident history, va output D1 da duoc load day du chua. Neu mot trong cac dependency local nay fail, service tra `503` thay vi nhan traffic that. Em chon FastAPI thay vi Flask vi FastAPI cho Pydantic validation, async middleware, va response model ro rang. Em khong chon BentoML vi bai nay khong phai model-serving thuần ML; no la serve ca pipeline correlation + RCA nen FastAPI linh hoat hon va overhead nhe hon.
